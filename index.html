✨ Mods & Features

A selection of hand-picked mods to ensure a fun gaming experience

⚙️ Technology

  • Create: The mechanical heart, with expansions such as Steam 'n' Rails, New Age, Enchantment Industry, Crafts & Additions and Slice & Dice.
  • Applied Energistics 2: For digital and logistics management, supported by Wireless Terminals, Extended AE, AdvancedAE and Infinite Storage Cell.
  • Modern Industrialization: Advanced Industry, complete with add-ons such as Nuclearization and Extended Industrialization.

🏡 Decor & Build

  • Create Deco & Interiors: To aesthetically enrich your bases.
  • Copycats+ & Blocks & Bogies: For new architectural shapes and details.
  • Bits 'n' Bobs: Extra finishing touches for your builds.
  • AdoraBuild: Structures: New and fascinating structures to explore around the world.

🌾 Exploration

  • Biomes O' Plenty: Breathtaking world generation, powered by TerraBlender.
  • Farmer's Delight: It greatly expands farming and cooking, complemented by Vegan Delight and Create: Food.
  • Traveler's Backpack: Spacious backpacks for longer and more adventurous trips.

  • FerriteCore

  • EMI & Addons
