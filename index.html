⚙️ Server Rules & Sanctions ⚙️

1. Be Polite

Keep cursing to a minimum and avoid insulting others. Respect space: don't build too close to neighbors.

Punishment: 3rd recall = Permanent Ban.

2. No Hacks

No cheats, bugs, or exploits. Report any bugs to staff immediately.

Punishment: Cheats = Ban. 1st dupe = Item removal. 2nd dupe = Perma Ban.

3. Zero Tolerance

No heavy curse words (e.g., N-word). No racism or racist remarks in chat or voice calls.

Punishment: Immediate Permanent Ban.

4. Social Rules

No spamming. No direct messages (DMs) to staff or players without permission; use tickets.

Spam: 2 rec. = 5d Mute | DMs: 3 rec. = 2d Ban.

5. Advertising

Do not share any links, advertisements, or promotions without asking for permission first.

Punishment: 2nd recall = 5 day Ban.

6. Grief & Steal

Do not grief or steal. If a creeper explodes, repair the damage and inform the owner.

Stealing: 2nd time = Ban. Grief: Must rebuild damage.

7. Server Performance

Do not lag the server intentionally or unintentionally. Turn off big Create factories when you log off.

Punishment: 3rd recall = 1 day Ban. Intentional lag = Permanent Ban.

✨ Mods & Features

A selection of hand-picked mods to ensure a fun gaming experience

⚙️ Technology

  • Create: The mechanical heart, with expansions such as Steam 'n' Rails, New Age, Enchantment Industry, Crafts & Additions and Slice & Dice.
  • Applied Energistics 2: For digital and logistics management, supported by Wireless Terminals, Extended AE, AdvancedAE and Infinite Storage Cell.
  • Modern Industrialization: Advanced Industry, complete with add-ons such as Nuclearization and Extended Industrialization.

🏡 Decor & Build

  • Create Deco & Interiors: To aesthetically enrich your bases.
  • Copycats+ & Blocks & Bogies: For new architectural shapes and details.
  • Bits 'n' Bobs: Extra finishing touches for your builds.
  • AdoraBuild: Structures: New and fascinating structures to explore around the world.

🌾 Exploration

  • Biomes O' Plenty: Breathtaking world generation, powered by TerraBlender.
  • Farmer's Delight: It greatly expands farming and cooking, complemented by Vegan Delight and Create: Food.
  • Traveler's Backpack: Spacious backpacks for longer and more adventurous trips.

⚡ Performance & Quality of Life

To ensure a smooth experience we use NeoForge with the best optimization mods:

  • Embeddium & Extras
  • FerriteCore
  • ModernFix
  • EMI & Addons
  • Xaero's Minimap
  • Simple Voice Chat
